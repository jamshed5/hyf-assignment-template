Chapli Kebab Recipe

Ingredients (Makes 6–8 kebabs)

500g minced beef or lamb (or mix)

1 medium onion, finely chopped

2–3 green chilies, finely chopped

2–3 tomatoes, chopped (or 2 tbsp tomato paste)

2–3 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped

1–2 tsp crushed garlic

1 tsp crushed ginger

1 tsp red chili flakes or powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp cumin powder

1 tsp garam masala

1 tsp crushed pomegranate seeds (anardana) – optional but authentic

1 egg

3–4 tbsp cornmeal or gram flour (to bind)

Salt to taste

Oil for frying
